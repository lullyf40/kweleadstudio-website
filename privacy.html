1. Who We Are

KweLead Studio and SODIAX are products of Luffra Group LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We provide wellness studio management software and behavioral health intelligence services. For privacy questions, contact us at lullyfritzf.es@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Identity

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Profile photo, language preference
  • Organization name and billing address

Health & Wellness Data (SODIAX)

  • Mood check-ins, PHQ-9/GAD-7 assessment responses
  • Biometric device readings (heart rate, blood pressure, weight, glucose) via Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)
  • Clinical notes, session transcripts, crisis event records
  • Substance use and mental health screening responses

Location Data

  • General location for finding nearby wellness studios and healthy places via Mapbox
  • Location is only accessed with your explicit permission

Device Data

  • Device type, operating system, browser
  • IP address, session identifiers
  • RPM-connected device readings and sync timestamps

Payment Information

  • Billing details processed securely by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Booking history, appointment records

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate KweLead Studio and SODIAX services
  • Process bookings, payments, and subscription management
  • Deliver Remote Patient Monitoring alerts and clinical insights
  • Send appointment reminders via email, SMS, and WhatsApp
  • Generate insurance billing claims (CPT codes 99453–99458)
  • Improve platform features and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

5. Health Data & HIPAA

SODIAX handles sensitive behavioral health information. We treat all health data with strict confidentiality. Health data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Accessible only to authorized practitioners and the patient
  • Never sold or shared with advertisers
  • Retained only as long as necessary for care or legal compliance
